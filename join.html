Be Part of the Show

The Triple Crown Sports Podcast is built around community. We do not just talk at our listeners—we talk with them. By joining our email list, you get early access to new episodes, weekly recap notes, and special polls that shape the direction of the show. When we choose games to feature or topics to debate, your input matters.

We also highlight listener questions and takes on air. Joining the community gives you priority when we select mailbag topics, fan-submitted segments, and live call-in opportunities during special episodes. If you have ever wanted to hear your take discussed on a sports show, this is your chance to step into the spotlight.

We respect your time and your inbox. Our goal is not to spam you but to send one focused email each week that recaps the best insights, highlights can’t-miss segments, and gives you a heads-up about what is coming next. If you ever want to step back, you can unsubscribe quickly and easily.
